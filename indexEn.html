Nearby sites and activities

Baños de vilo

The baños de vilo It is a water pool or Arab spa that has extraordinary hydrogen sulfide, magnesium-calcium and nitrogenous waters, which gush at 21 degrees Celsius and whose healing properties are especially indicated for skin diseases.
This is located in Guaro about 2 km from Periana.

Swamp de la Viñuela

A reservoir with a capacity of 170 hm³ and has 565 hectares, is the reservoir with the highest capacity in Malaga. You can practice water sports, hiking or have a barbecue inthe romería of Periana. You can also find restaurants and there is also a nearby campsite. This is located near the town the Viñuela end the Romanes.

Suspension bridge El saltillo

A 50m long bridge at a height of 78m that offers incredible views, it is the 3rd largest bridge in Spain in natural spaces and here in addition to this bridge there are also several hiking trails through the mountains.
This is located in the village of Canillas de aceituno.

Ancient trees

On this route you will find olive trees with a perimeter of 7.4, 5.5, 6.5 m among others, which correspond to 1500, 1000 and 1200 years old. On your way you can enjoy the mark that time has left on these olive trees, turning them into true artistic sculptures. This is located in the village of Periana.
